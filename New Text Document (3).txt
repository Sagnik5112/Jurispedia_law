    <h2>About JurisPedia</h2>
    <p>
      JurisPedia is a premier academic initiative under the banner of Law & Beyond, designed to foster excellence in legal education and professional development. Built on the principles of accessibility, academic rigor, and mentorship, JurisPedia serves as a one-stop platform for law aspirants, students, and professionals. Our holistic approach integrates classroom teaching with skill-based learning, offering tailored guidance for entrance examinations, semester preparation, and judicial service exams. Through curated mentorship, internship linkages, and expert-led sessions, JurisPedia bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, empowering learners to navigate the legal landscape with confidence and clarity.
    </p>

    <h2>About the Founder</h2>
    <p>
      Mr. Sagnik Chakraborty, the visionary founder of JurisPedia, is a distinguished academician and legal educator with a robust background in both theory and practice. An alumnus of the Department of Law, University of Burdwan, Mr. Chakraborty is currently a Research Scholar at the National Law University, Tripura, specialising in Corporate Law, Criminal Law and Forensic Sciences. With a deep commitment to inclusive and transformative education, he founded JurisPedia to democratize access to quality legal training.
